You, as a planner, are responsible of the master product programming of the polyestiren (RED and BLUE).

This means that you will decide on how much to produce for 2 products.

Your performance is vital for the economic success of the enterprise.

DECISION

Your task is to decide the ammount of thread (kg), of each color, to be produced in each period.

Sequence of Events

(1) The initial Inventories are observred for a period.

(2) You then DECIDE on the production lotsize of the period.

(3) Demand of the period is revealed

(4) Demand will be covered by the current stock plus the production lotsize just produced.

(5) If all the stock plus the production lotsize is not enough to cover the current demand, then the uncovered demand is considered to be back-ordered, meaning that you still need to fulfill the demand at a later stage.
