The objective of your decision in production is to minimize the total cost. The costs can come from inventory holding, production and backorder in each period.

There are no setup costs and the time to produce a Kg is 0 (no Lead time).

1. Inventory Holding Costs

When items are stored across periods, a unit inventory cost is incurred per item. This cost is associated to financial cost and inventory management. In each period you will be reminded of the holding cost.

2. Production Costs

For producing the product, a production unit cost per kilogram is incurred.

3. Backorder Cost

This cost refers to the cost of not being able to supply the demand. All backordered demand is considered as negative inventory and has priority to be covered in the next period. For each kilogram of product backordered (negative inventory) a penalty cost is incurred.

All these costs will appear at the top corner of the screen.
