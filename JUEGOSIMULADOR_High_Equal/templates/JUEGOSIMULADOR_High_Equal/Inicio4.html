The objective of the decision in production is to minimize the total cost. The costs can come from inventory holding, production and backorder in each period.

There are no setup costs and the time to produce a Kg is 0 (no Lead time).

1. Inventory Holding Costs

This cost is the one paid in order to storage the products that were not sold in each period. In each period you will be reminded of the holding cost.

2. Production Costs

For each kilogram of product produced, there is a cost attached, this cost will be shown in each decision page.

3. Backorder Cost

This cost refers to the cost of not being able to supply the demand. And so the ammount of demand not supplied is stacked as negative inventory and has a cost for each period not supplied.
